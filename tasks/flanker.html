Flanker Task

Task Description

Definition: The Flanker Task is a psychological test used to assess attention and cognitive control. It measures how well a person can respond to a target stimulus while ignoring surrounding distractors.

Instructions: When you are ready press the start button below. You will see 5 arrows in a row. Press the arrow key (on the keyboard) that matches the direction of the central arrow as quickly and accurately as possible. Sometimes the other arrows will point in the same (congruent) or opposite direction (incongruent).

The task consists of 20 trials.

After completing all trials, your results will be displayed.
